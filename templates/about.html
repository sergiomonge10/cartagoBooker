Welcome to Booker

Booker was born in 2013 through the initiative of some friends book lovers who wanted to create a portal where users could share their writings by ebooks.

The site works as a social network dedicated to books intended for anyone willing to fulfill the dream of publish their own books and to know what others think about it.
